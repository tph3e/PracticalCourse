R-RRA completed all $380/380$ fixed-route instances, while R-SHQ completed
$379/380$. R-SHQ reduced the mean cycle time from $29.2187$ h to $18.1068$ h
and the Gini coefficient from $0.5327$ to $0.4738$. Its weighted fairness was
also lower, decreasing from $0.0053$ to $0.0032$.

The mean waiting time was slightly higher for R-SHQ: $27.9555$ min compared
with $27.2885$ min for R-RRA. Lower Gini and weighted-fairness values indicate
a more balanced workload.

R-SHQ provides the lowest cycle time and fairness values among completed routes, whereas R-RRA completes
one additional route. Because cycle time is conditional on completion, the comparison is descriptive rather than
evidence of statistical dominance. Per-seed results are reported in
App.~\ref{app:joao-fixed-replay-details}, and the full comparison is shown in
