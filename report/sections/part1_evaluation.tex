\subsubsection{Resource Model Faithfulness}\label{ssub:res-eval}
\textit{(Aldo Patrone)}\quad
The resource models are validated against the real BPIC-17 log
(Sec.~\ref{subsec:resources}, Table~\ref{tab:resource_eval}): the learned calendars
cover $96.4\%$ of events against $72.0\%$ for the fixed interval, while role discovery
keeps all $26$ activities executable and adds $872$ role-based permissions. The full
simulated-versus-real evaluation (utilization, waiting and cycle times) is produced in Part II.
